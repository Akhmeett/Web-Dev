Beshbarmak

  • Time: 3 hours
  • Serves: 5 people
Ingredients Quantity
meat 1.5 kg
flour 1 kg
onion 2 pieces
salt to taste

Step 1: Boil the meat in water until it becomes soft. Prepare the dough, cut it into pieces, and cook it in the meat broth. Serve with sliced onions.

Baursak

  • Time: 1.5 hours
  • Serves: 6 people
Ingredients Quantity
flour 1 kg
milk 500 ml
yeast 1 teaspoon
oil for frying

Step 1: Mix all ingredients, make the dough, cut into small pieces, and fry in hot oil until golden.

Plov

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves: 6 people
Ingredients Quantity
rice 1 kg
meat 1 kg
carrot 500 g
onion 2 pieces

Step 1: Fry meat with onions, add carrots, then rice and water. Cook until rice is ready.

Source: Википедия
